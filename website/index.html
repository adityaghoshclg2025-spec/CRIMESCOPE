Process

Five steps from evidence
to verdict

CrimeScope's swarm intelligence pipeline transforms raw, unstructured evidence into a ranked probability map of criminal hypotheses — with full causal reasoning you can audit.

01
Evidence Ingestion
Upload PDFs, images, CCTV stills, audio transcripts, spreadsheets, or raw text. CrimeScope parses and chunks every modality into its evidence graph.
02
Ontology Construction
An ontology agent extracts entities — people, locations, objects, events, timestamps — and constructs a typed knowledge graph stored in Zep's memory layer.
03
Swarm Deployment
1,000 specialised AI agents — Witness Analysts, Physical Evidence Specialists, Timeline Reconstructors, Motive Profilers — disperse across the evidence graph simultaneously.
04
Hypothesis Convergence
Agents report findings back to a convergence layer. Contradictions are flagged. Corroborating chains strengthen probability scores. A consensus hypothesis emerges.
05
Forensic Report
A structured report is generated with ranked hypotheses, confidence scores, evidence citations, timeline reconstructions, and identified investigative leads.
